# MinuteEarth Needs Your Support

[00:00] Hey there! I'm Kate, and we're MinuteEarth. We use stick figures, science, and puns to explain what we know about our planet, and how we know it. More often than not, when we tell people that we make a living by producing videos and distributing them for free online, their first question is, 'so how do you get money?' And the truth is, while we have some consistent revenue coming in from our viewers and from ads, we’ve gotten really lucky with sponsorships and other funding opportunities. But the Internet is a constantly changing ecosystem, and over the last year, some of our bigger sources of funding have greatly diminished and others have disappeared, leaving us unsure whether we’ll be able to keep making MinuteEarth.

[00:38] And that’s really sad - we love making videos, but what we love even more is that YOU love our videos. We love being able to make videos for a living, and for you! And that's why we want to keep making them...but we need your help. If you support rigorous, fact-checked science videos with a side of silliness, will you contribute whatever you can - even if it's just a dollar - for every new video we make? You can do it on Patreon, an incredible website where people like you support creators like us. If you already support us on Patreon, you can help us by raising the monthly maximum on your pledge - but in any case, thank you for your continued support!

[01:09] We want to post videos more often, and to spend more time talking with you and finding out what topics you guys are curious about. If you want to be a part of all of this, please support us on Patreon.com/MinuteEarth. Thank you for helping MinuteEarth “stick” around.
