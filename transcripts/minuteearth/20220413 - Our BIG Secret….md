# Our BIG Secret…

[00:00] Hi, this is Kate from MinuteEarth - and our corner of the cosmos is getting a little less minute; we have three new additions to the MinuteUniverse that we’re excited to share with you. The first is MinuteFood, a new channel all about the science of deliciousness. Every week or two, I’ll take you into my own kitchen and use science to answer all sorts of culinary queries, like “Why does garlic sometimes taste SO strong?” “What is tofu, and how do I cook it?” and “How the heck does a microwave actually work?” Then, there’s MinuteMinis, a new shorts channel featuring highlights from all the Minute channels, along with original minis about everything from frozen frogs to space photography to supervolcanoes. MinuteMinis finally gives you what you’ve been wanting for years: Minute videos that are actually a minute long.

[00:51] And finally, after frustratingly limited availability due to supply-chain problems, the MinuteEarth Explains book is now available in online bookstores all around the world. Now you can hold our gorgeous illustrations in your hand and physically flip through two dozen of our favorite stories about the amazing science that happens all around us. All these new projects are, of course, in addition to the amazing stuff that’s already in our planetary system: MinutePhysics, where you’ll find all sorts of fascinating physics explainers - and, well, Henry - and MinuteLabs, the home of Jasper’s fun science interactives.

[01:28] So, just like the real universe, the MinuteUniverse is expanding - and we’re sure you’re going to have a blast exploring it all. Click below to subscribe to MinuteFood and MinuteMinis - and to grab a copy of our new book…it’ll just take a minute.
